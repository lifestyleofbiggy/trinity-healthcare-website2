About Trinity

Building better healthcare teams through dedicated staffing solutions

Who We Are

Trinity was born out of a simple realisation: healthcare facilities deserve better support when it comes to staffing.

The Problem

Our founder, with over 10 years of experience in the healthcare space, saw first-hand how organisations struggled with recruitment. Roles stayed open for too long, compliance checks were inconsistent, and managers were left juggling payroll and benefits on top of everything else.

The system wasn't working — and it was costing both providers and patients.

Our Response

Instead of accepting this as "the way things are," our founder decided to build Trinity as a better option. A staffing partner that does more than match CVs to job descriptions — one that actually solves the stress points leaders face every day.

Our Solution

At Trinity, we created a model designed to make healthcare staffing simple, reliable, and safe.

We offer two tailored packages:

Standard Package

Focuses on finding and vetting qualified candidates

Premium Package

Takes the entire staffing burden off your shoulders, including payroll and benefits
